Key Features





User Authentication and Profiles:



User Registration and Login: Secure user registration and login functionality for customers.



Profile Management: Customers can manage their profiles with personal information and preferences.



Service Management:



Service Listings: The salon can list all the services they offer, including descriptions, durations, and prices.



Manage Availability: The salon can set their working hours and manage availability for each service.



Staff Management:



Staff Profiles: Detailed profiles for each staff member, including their specializations and availability.



Assign Services: Assign specific services to staff members based on their skills and schedules.



Appointment Booking:



Search and Book: Customers can view available slots and book appointments based on the salon’s schedule.



Booking Confirmation: Automated confirmation messages via email or SMS.



Appointment Details: Detailed information about the appointment including date, time, service, and assigned staff.



Reminders and Notifications:



Appointment Reminders: Automated reminders via email for upcoming appointments (use cron job) .



Appointment Management:



Rescheduling and Cancellations: Users can reschedule or cancel appointments based on predefined policies.



Payment Integration:



Online Payments: Integration with payment gateways (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) for online payments at the time of booking.



Reviews and Feedback:



Customer Reviews: Customers can leave reviews and feedback for services received.



Staff Response: Staff can respond to reviews to maintain their reputation.



Admin Dashboard:



User Management: Admins can manage customer accounts.



Appointment Management: Admins can view, edit, and manage all appointments.



 

Technical Requirements

Backend





Framework: Node.js with Express 



Database: SQL DB



Authentication: JWT (JSON Web Tokens) for user authentication



Notification Service: Integration with email and SMS services (e.g., SendGrid)



Payment Integration: Integration with payment gateways like Stripe or Razorpay

Frontend





Framework: React.js (if you know) or HTML/CSS/JS




Additional Tools





Version Control: Git with GitHub or GitLab



Deployment: AWS
